# 🏥 ResistAI — Antibiotic Resistance AI App

A multi-agent AI powered app that predicts antibiotic resistance (AMR) and recommends safer alternatives, built as a hackathon project to tackle one of WHO's top global health threats.

---

## 🚨 Problem Statement

Antimicrobial Resistance (AMR) is one of the biggest global health threats — bacteria are becoming resistant to antibiotics faster than new ones are developed. Doctors often prescribe antibiotics without knowing if the bacteria is already resistant, leading to treatment failure and worsening resistance.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

ResistAI is an AI-powered app that:
- Predicts whether a bacteria strain is resistant to a given antibiotic
- Recommends safer, effective antibiotic alternatives
- Tracks patient medicine adherence via QR codes
- Uses multi-agent AI architecture for intelligent decision making

## 🏗️ System Architecture

**Agent 1 — Resistance Predictor**
Analyses patient data and predicts AMR risk

**Agent 2 — Antibiotic Recommender**
Suggests safer antibiotic alternatives based on resistance profile

**Agent 3 — Adherence Tracker**
Tracks if patient is taking medicine on time via QR code scanning
